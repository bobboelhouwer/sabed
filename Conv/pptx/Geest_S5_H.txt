 Ethnomethodologische benaderingen
• Garfinkel, Sacks
• Sacks’ Membership Category Analysis
• gebruik van categorisering door sprekers/schrijvers
o

“One of the features of description and recognizability in conversation is the
display of categories and the methodical process of categorization. In
Sacks’s famous example – ‘The baby cried. The mommy picked it up’ –
these considerations are illuminated by an analytical consideration of how
we make sense of the story. In terms of Sacks’s example we understand
the story in terms of the ‘mommy’ picking up her ‘baby’ in response to the
baby crying. For Sacks, we understand this story in this way because we
associate the categories of ‘baby’ and ‘mommy’ with the membership
categorization device ‘the family’.” (Housley and Fitzgerald, 2002: 61)
